Suomi.fi e-Identification is a shared identification service for public administration e-services. It will make identification secure and easy. You can use the identification method of your preference. Suomi.fi e-Identification uses a brokering service. The brokering service for OP Identity Service is provided by OP Group banks and OP Corporate Bank. At the time of identification, the user’s personal identity code and name are transmitted to the service provider. Privacy noticeLink opens in a new window
